====== Tabbing ======
Created Thursday 10 March 2016

* Open a new tab: 
{{{code: lang="sh" linenumbers="True"
:tabnew
}}}

* Switch between tabs: 
{{{code: lang="sh" linenumbers="True"
gt #or
:tabn #next, or
:tabp #prev
}}}
