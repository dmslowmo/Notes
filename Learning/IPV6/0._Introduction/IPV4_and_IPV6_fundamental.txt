====== IPV4 and IPV6 fundamental ======
Created Tuesday 15 March 2016

===== IPV4 =====
* Address space is 2^32 (4+ billion)
* IANA = Internet Assigned Number Authority
* Format: dotted, 4-octets
* Unicast, Multicast, Broadcast, Anycast

===== IPV6 =====
* expanded addressing
* header simplification
* extension
* flow labels
* authentication
* Address space is 2^128
* Will be standar network protocol, but co-exist with IPV4
* Unicast, Multicast, Anycast
	* No Broadcast because it is practically Multicast
* Address format: 8 hexadectets, or hextets, or a quibble (a quad nibble) separated by a colon
