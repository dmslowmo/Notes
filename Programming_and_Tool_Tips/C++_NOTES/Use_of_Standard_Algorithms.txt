====== Use of Standard Algorithms ======
Created Monday 29 February 2016

* Expressive and readable
* Edge cases are remembered
* Easy to swap in a different container
* May even be a performance boost

* See:
	* http://www.cplusplus.com/reference/algorithm/
	* http://msdn.microsoft.com/em-us/library/chzkfc23.aspx

* back_inserter (like v.push_back())
* begin(), end()
* count, count_if
* for_each
* remove_if
* and many more
